Why Volunteer?

Volunteering is not only a rewarding experience, but it also helps build your network and enhance your soft skills like time management, mentoring, organization & leadership, all while learning about a great cause and making a real positive difference & impact on the community!

Purpose of volunteer program

Virtual English Communication Program is one of the relatively new enrichment activities offered to Agaram students to augment their learning & career goals. â€¢ The objective of the program is to coach our students. with the required everyday tools & techniques to practice and improve their English communication skills and build confidence that in-turn help unlock their true potential.

How does the program work?

Two volunteers will team up to coach a batch of 10-15 friends. Agaram students, leveraging a pre-defined curriculum. Each batch will virtually meet for 1-2 hours, once a week, for 12 weeks.Training will be provided for all new volunteers before their batch starts and they will also attend a couple of checkpoint meetings to assess progress & gather feedback during the program.

Volunteer's prerequisite

We are currently looking for 50 more volunteers who meet the following pre-requisites to coach our next wave of batches starting Aug/Sep timeframe: Must have fluency in Tamil & English languages. Preferably completed PG or equivalent, or with relevant prior work experience. Stable internet connection & personal computer, to join the weekly remote sessions. Be ready to commit -2 hours per week for 12 weeks starting Aug/Sep 22. Be available for a 2-hours training prior to the start of the program. Above all, bring your smile!
