What is Agaram?

Established on 25th september, 2006 by renowned Film Actor Mr. Suriya, Agaram aims to provide quality education to the under-privileged an deserving students. We truly believe in leveraging the power of education to close the opportunity & achievement gaps faced by socio economically backward first-generation students. Thanks to all our donors & volunteers since 2010, whose kind support has fulfilled the college education aspirations for 2418 students from under-served communities so far, with 1740 more students currently pursuing their college education dreams through Agaram Vidhai initiative.

What is MAM?

It locates at Trichy. MAM is a conglomerate of several collegiate institutions. Both MAM College of Engineering, MAM College of Engineering and Technology and MAM Architecture are associated with Agaram Foundation.
